Online To-let

The Online Toilet Room Rental System is a modern digital platform designed to make public sanitation facilities more accessible and convenient. With the help of this system, users can easily search, locate, and book nearby toilets in advance through their mobile or web application. This solution not only saves time but also ensures cleanliness and safety, as service providers can regularly update the status of hygiene and availability.

Mobile Application

My project is a Durga Puja mobile application developed for Android devices. This app is designed to provide users with detailed information about Durga Puja, including the significance of the festival, rituals, dates, and cultural events. It features interactive sections like photo galleries, live updates of Puja pandals, and notifications about important celebrations. The app also includes a user-friendly interface, making it easy for devotees and festival enthusiasts to explore Puja schedules, nearby events, and traditional recipes. Developed using Android.
